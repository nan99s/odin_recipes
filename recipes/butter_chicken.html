Butter Chicken

Description

Follow this recipe for a quick, easy butter chicken that is brimming with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken thight fillets, chopped
  • 1/2 x 312g jar Patak's Butter Chicken Paste
  • 400ml can coconut cream
  • 350g green beans, halved
  • Steamed basmati rice
  • Basil sprigs
  • Naan bread

Steps

  1. Heat a saucepan over medium heat. Add the chicken and Paste, stir for 2-3 minutes until chicken is golden.
  2. Reserve 2 tbsp of coconut cream. Add cream to the pan with 1/2 cup water. Reduce to low heat. Cover and cook for 6-8 minutes.
  3. Add the beans, cook for another 4 minutes. Season.
  4. Divide the rice among serving bowls, top with curry. Sprinkle basil and drizzle with reserved coconut cream. Serve with naan.
